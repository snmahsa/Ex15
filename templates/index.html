About Iris Flowers

Iris flowers are renowned for their stunning beauty and diversity. With over 300 species and numerous hybrids, they come in a variety of colors and shapes, making them a favorite among gardeners and nature lovers.

Their unique features, such as the shape of their petals and the length of their stems, allow us to classify them into different species. In this system, you can input the characteristics of an iris flower, and we will predict its species for you!
